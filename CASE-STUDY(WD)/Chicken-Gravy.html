Chicken and Gravy

Crispy fried chicken paired with rich and creamy gravy.

CATEGORY
MAIN DISH
CALORIES
350 kcal per serving
TIME
1 hour

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 pieces chicken thighs or breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp flour (for gravy)
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1.

Season the chicken with salt and pepper. Coat the chicken with flour on both sides.

Step 2.

In a large skillet, heat some oil over medium heat and fry the chicken until golden brown and crispy on both sides. Remove and set aside.

Step 3.

In the same skillet, melt butter. Add 1 tablespoon of flour and cook, whisking constantly, for 1 minute to form a roux.

Step 4.

Slowly whisk in chicken broth and bring to a simmer. Let it cook for 3-4 minutes, then stir in the heavy cream and cook until the gravy thickens.

Step 5.

Pour the gravy over the fried chicken and serve immediately. Enjoy with mashed potatoes or your favorite side dish!
