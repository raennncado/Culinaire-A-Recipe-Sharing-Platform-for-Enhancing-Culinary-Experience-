Kare-kare

A traditional Filipino stew with peanut sauce and assorted meats.

CATEGORY
MAIN DISH
CALORIES
450 kcal per serving
TIME
1 hour

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 kg beef shank, cut into chunks
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup annatto powder
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 1 bunch string beans, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 banana heart, sliced (optional)
  • 1/2 cup bagoong (fermented shrimp paste)

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1.

Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onions and garlic, cooking until fragrant.

Step 2.

Add the beef shank and cook until browned on all sides.

Step 3.

Pour in the beef broth and bring to a boil. Lower the heat and simmer for 45 minutes to an hour, or until the beef is tender.

Step 4.

Stir in the peanut butter and annatto powder, mixing until the sauce is smooth and creamy.

Step 5.

Add the eggplant, string beans, and banana heart (if using). Continue simmering for another 5-10 minutes until the vegetables are tender.

Step 6.

Serve the Kare-kare with a side of bagoong and steamed rice.
