Mushroom Pizza with Black Olives

Thin crust pizza topped with mushrooms and black olives.

CATEGORY
MAIN DISH
CALORIES
250 kcal per slice
TIME
30 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 cup black olives, sliced
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1.

Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C). Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.

Step 2.

Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.

Step 3.

Scatter the sliced mushrooms and black olives over the sauce. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the toppings.

Step 4.

Sprinkle the oregano, black pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using) on top. Drizzle the olive oil over the pizza.

Step 5.

Transfer the pizza to a baking sheet or pizza stone and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Step 6.

Remove from the oven and let it cool for a minute. Slice and serve!
