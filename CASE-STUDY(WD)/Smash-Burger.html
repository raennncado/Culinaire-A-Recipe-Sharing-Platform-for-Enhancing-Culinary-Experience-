Smash Burger

A juicy, crispy burger with a perfect crispy edge and tender center.

CATEGORY
Main Dish
CALORIES
400 kcal per serving
TIME
30 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 lb ground beef (80% lean, 20% fat)
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 4 slices pickles
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • Ketchup and mustard (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1.

Preheat a cast iron skillet or griddle over medium-high heat and add a tablespoon of vegetable oil.

Step 2.

While the skillet is heating up, divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and roll them into balls. Season with salt and pepper.

Step 3.

Once the skillet is hot, place a beef ball onto the skillet and smash it down with a spatula to form a thin patty. Cook for 2-3 minutes until crispy, then flip and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.

Step 4.

During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheddar cheese on top of the patty and let it melt.

Step 5.

Toast the buns on the skillet until golden brown, about 1-2 minutes.

Step 6.

Assemble the burger by placing the cooked patty with cheese on the bottom bun, adding pickles, onions, and any other desired toppings.

Step 7.

Top with the other half of the bun and serve immediately.
