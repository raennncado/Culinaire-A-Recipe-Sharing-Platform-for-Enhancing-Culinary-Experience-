Filet Mignon

Tender and juicy steak cooked to perfection.

CATEGORY
LUNCH / DINNER
CALORIES
350 kcal per serving
TIME
25 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 filet mignon steaks (6 oz each)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 sprig rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1.

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Season the steaks with salt and pepper on both sides.

Step 2.

Heat olive oil in a cast iron skillet over medium-high heat. Add the steaks and sear for 2-3 minutes on each side until browned.

Step 3.

Add butter, garlic, and rosemary to the skillet. Spoon the melted butter over the steaks as they cook for another 2-3 minutes.

Step 4.

Transfer the skillet to the oven and cook for an additional 5-7 minutes for medium-rare, or longer for your desired doneness.

Step 5.

Remove the steaks from the skillet and let them rest for 5 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh parsley.
