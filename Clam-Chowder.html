Clam Chowder

Rich and creamy soup with clams, potatoes, and celery.

CATEGORY
SOUP
CALORIES
280 kcal per serving
TIME
40 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups fresh clams, shelled and chopped
  • 2 cups potatoes, diced
  • 1/2 cup celery, diced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1.

In a large pot, melt butter over medium heat. Add onions, garlic, celery, and potatoes. Sauté until softened, about 5 minutes.

Step 2.

Add chicken broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes until the potatoes are tender.

Step 3.

Stir in clams and heavy cream. Simmer for another 5 minutes, until the soup is heated through.

Step 4.

Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot and enjoy.
