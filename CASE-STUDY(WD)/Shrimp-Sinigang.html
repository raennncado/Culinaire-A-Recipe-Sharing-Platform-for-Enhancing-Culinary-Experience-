Shrimp Sinigang

A sour Filipino soup with shrimp, tamarind, and fresh vegetables.

CATEGORY
SOUP
CALORIES
180 kcal per serving
TIME
45 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 medium tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 1 packet tamarind soup base
  • 1 cup long green beans, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 cup water spinach (kangkong) or other leafy greens
  • 1 medium eggplant, sliced
  • 2-3 pieces of chili (optional)
  • Salt and fish sauce to taste
  • 6 cups water

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1.

In a large pot, combine the shrimp, tomatoes, onion, and water. Bring to a boil over medium heat.

Step 2.

Once boiling, add the tamarind soup base and stir well. Let it cook for about 5 minutes.

Step 3.

Add the green beans, eggplant, and chili (if using). Let it cook for another 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender.

Step 4.

Season with salt and fish sauce to taste. Adjust the sourness by adding more tamarind if needed.

Step 5.

Finally, add the water spinach and cook for another 2 minutes, then remove from heat.

Step 6.

Serve hot with steamed rice and enjoy!
