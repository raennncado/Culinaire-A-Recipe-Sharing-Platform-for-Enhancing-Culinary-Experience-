Tomato Salad

Fresh and vibrant tomato salad with a zesty dressing, perfect as a side dish.

CATEGORY
Salad
CALORIES
120 kcal per serving
TIME
15 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cucumber, diced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1.

In a large bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, red onion, and cucumber.

Step 2.

In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil and balsamic vinegar. Season with salt and pepper.

Step 3.

Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.

Step 4.

Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving. Enjoy immediately!
