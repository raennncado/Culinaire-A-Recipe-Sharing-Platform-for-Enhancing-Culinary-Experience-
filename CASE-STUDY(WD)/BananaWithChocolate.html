Banana With Chocolate Sauce

Sweet bananas drizzled with a rich chocolate sauce.

CATEGORY
DESSERT
CALORIES
180 kcal
TIME
20 mins

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 100g dark or milk chocolate
  • 1 tsp coconut oil (optional)
  • Sprinkles, crushed nuts, or shredded coconut (optional)
  • Wooden skewers or popsicle sticks

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1.

Peel bananas, cut into halves, and insert skewers or popsicle sticks.

Step 2.

Melt the chocolate in a microwave or double boiler; add coconut oil if desired.

Step 3.

Dip each banana piece into melted chocolate, covering evenly.

Step 4.

Optional: Add sprinkles, nuts, or coconut while chocolate is wet.

Step 5.

Place bananas on a parchment-lined tray and freeze for 20–30 minutes.

Step 6.

Serve frozen and enjoy!
