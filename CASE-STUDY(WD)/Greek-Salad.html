Greek Salad

A vibrant salad with fresh cucumbers, tomatoes, olives, and feta cheese.

CATEGORY
SALAD
CALORIES
150 kcal per serving
TIME
15 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 2 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1.

In a large bowl, combine the cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, olives, and feta cheese.

Step 2.

In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper.

Step 3.

Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine. Serve immediately.
