Cheesecake

A smooth and creamy dessert with a graham cracker crust.

CATEGORY
DESSERT
CALORIES
250 kcal per serving
TIME
1 hour

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 3 packages (8 oz each) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup sugar (for sour cream topping)

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1.

Preheat oven to 325°F (163°C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan with butter.

Step 2.

In a medium bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Press mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan to form the crust. Bake for 10 minutes, then remove from oven and let cool.

Step 3.

In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth. Add sugar and vanilla extract, and beat until combined.

Step 4.

Beat in eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.

Step 5.

Pour the cream cheese mixture over the cooled crust. Bake for 50-55 minutes, or until the center is just set.

Step 6.

While the cheesecake is baking, mix sour cream and sugar in a bowl. Once the cheesecake is done, spread sour cream topping over the top and return to the oven for 10 more minutes.

Step 7.

Allow the cheesecake to cool completely before refrigerating for at least 4 hours. Slice and serve chilled!
