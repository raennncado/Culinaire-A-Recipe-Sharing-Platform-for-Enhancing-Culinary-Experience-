Chicken Noodle Soup

A hearty soup with tender chicken, noodles, and vegetables.

CATEGORY
SOUP
CALORIES
250 kcal per serving
TIME
45 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup carrots, diced
  • 1 cup celery, diced
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 1/2 cups egg noodles
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1.

Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions, carrots, celery, and garlic. Sauté until softened, about 5 minutes.

Step 2.

Add chicken broth and bay leaf to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and let it simmer for 15 minutes.

Step 3.

Add the cooked, shredded chicken and egg noodles. Simmer for another 10 minutes or until noodles are tender.

Step 4.

Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove the bay leaf and serve hot.
