Caesar Salad

Crisp lettuce tossed in Caesar dressing with croutons and parmesan.

CATEGORY
SALAD
CALORIES
230 kcal
TIME
15 mins

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups Romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1.

Wash and chop the Romaine lettuce into bite-sized pieces.

Step 2.

In a large bowl, toss the lettuce with Caesar dressing until evenly coated.

Step 3.

Top with grated Parmesan cheese and croutons.

Step 4.

Season with black pepper and toss again.

Step 5.

Serve immediately and enjoy!
