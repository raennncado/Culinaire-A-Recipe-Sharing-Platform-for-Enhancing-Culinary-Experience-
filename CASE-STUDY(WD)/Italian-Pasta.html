Italian Pasta

Classic pasta tossed in a rich and flavorful Italian sauce.

CATEGORY
MAIN DISH
CALORIES
350 kcal per serving
TIME
20 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • 200g pasta (spaghetti or penne)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes (400g)
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • Grated parmesan cheese

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1.

Cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

Step 2.

In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.

Step 3.

Add the crushed tomatoes, oregano, chili flakes (if using), salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes to allow the sauce to thicken and flavors to combine.

Step 4.

Mix the cooked pasta into the sauce, toss to coat evenly.

Step 5.

Serve the pasta topped with fresh basil and a sprinkle of grated parmesan cheese.
