Cassava Cake

A Filipino dessert made from grated cassava and coconut milk.

CATEGORY
DESSERT
CALORIES
180 kcal per serving
TIME
45 mins

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups grated cassava
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese (optional, for topping)

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1.

Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a baking dish with butter or cooking spray.

Step 2.

In a large mixing bowl, combine the grated cassava, coconut milk, sugar, condensed milk, melted butter, beaten egg, vanilla extract, and salt. Mix well.

Step 3.

Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish and smooth the top with a spatula.

Step 4.

Optional: Sprinkle grated cheese on top for a savory touch.

Step 5.

Bake for 30-35 minutes or until the top is golden and the center is set.

Step 6.

Allow to cool before slicing into squares. Serve and enjoy!
