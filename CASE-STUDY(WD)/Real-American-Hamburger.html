Real American Hamburger

A classic American burger with a juicy beef patty and fresh toppings.

CATEGORY
MAIN DISH
CALORIES
350 kcal per serving
TIME
25 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20 lean-to-fat ratio)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of American cheese
  • 4 lettuce leaves
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • Ketchup and mustard
  • Pickles (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1.

Preheat your grill or stovetop griddle over medium-high heat.

Step 2.

Season the ground beef with salt and pepper. Divide the meat into 4 equal portions and form into burger patties.

Step 3.

Place the patties on the grill or griddle. Cook for 4-5 minutes per side for medium doneness, or adjust the time to your preference.

Step 4.

Place a slice of cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking, allowing it to melt.

Step 5.

Toast the buns lightly on the grill or in a toaster.

Step 6.

Assemble the burgers by placing lettuce, tomato, and onions on the bottom half of each bun. Add the cooked patty with melted cheese and top with condiments like ketchup, mustard, and pickles if desired. Serve and enjoy!
