Lechon Kawali

Crispy fried pork belly served with a savory dipping sauce.

CATEGORY
MAIN DISH
CALORIES
650 kcal per serving
TIME
1 hour

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 kg pork belly
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • Oil for frying
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce (for dipping sauce)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (for dipping sauce)
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar (for dipping sauce)
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1.

In a pot, combine pork belly, soy sauce, onion, garlic, bay leaf, peppercorns, salt, vinegar, sugar, and water. Bring to a boil over medium heat, then lower the heat and simmer for 45 minutes until the pork is tender.

Step 2.

Remove the pork belly and let it cool. Once cool, cut into bite-sized pieces.

Step 3.

Heat oil in a deep pan or fryer over medium-high heat. Fry the pork pieces until golden brown and crispy, about 10-15 minutes. Remove from the oil and drain excess oil on paper towels.

Step 4.

For the dipping sauce, combine soy sauce, sugar, vinegar, and chili flakes (if using) in a small bowl. Stir to mix well.

Step 5.

Serve the crispy Lechon Kawali with the dipping sauce and enjoy!
