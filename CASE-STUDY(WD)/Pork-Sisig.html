Pork Sisig

A sizzling Filipino dish made of chopped pork and savory seasonings.

CATEGORY
MAIN DISH
CALORIES
300 kcal per serving
TIME
40 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 lb pork belly, diced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2-3 bird's eye chili peppers (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup calamansi juice (or lemon juice)
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 egg (optional, for topping)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground pepper

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1.

Boil the pork belly in water with a pinch of salt until tender. Drain and set aside.

Step 2.

Grill or fry the boiled pork belly until crispy and golden brown.

Step 3.

Chop the crispy pork into small pieces and set aside.

Step 4.

In a hot skillet, sauté onions, garlic, and chili peppers (if using) in oil until fragrant.

Step 5.

Add the chopped pork to the skillet and mix well. Pour in the soy sauce, vinegar, oyster sauce, and calamansi juice. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir-fry for a few minutes.

Step 6.

Top with mayonnaise and an optional egg (fried sunny-side up) before serving. Enjoy!
