Tasty Mozzarella Buns

Fluffy and cheesy buns, perfect for any meal or snack time.

CATEGORY
APPETIZER
CALORIES
150 kcal per bun
TIME
45 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (for greasing)
  • 1 egg wash (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1.

In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, and salt. Sprinkle the yeast on top and stir to combine.

Step 2.

Add warm milk, melted butter, and egg. Mix until a dough forms, then knead for 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.

Step 3.

Cover the dough with a clean towel and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.

Step 4.

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a baking sheet with olive oil.

Step 5.

After the dough has risen, divide it into equal portions and shape each portion into a ball. Place each ball on the greased baking sheet, spacing them apart.

Step 6.

Make a small indentation in the center of each dough ball and fill it with mozzarella cheese.

Step 7.

Brush the buns with egg wash for a golden finish and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the buns are golden brown.

Step 8.

Let the buns cool slightly before serving. Enjoy warm!
