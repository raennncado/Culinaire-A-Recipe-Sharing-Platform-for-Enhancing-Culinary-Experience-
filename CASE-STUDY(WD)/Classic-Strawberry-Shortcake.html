Classic Strawberry Shortcake

A delightful dessert with layers of sponge cake, strawberries, and cream.

CATEGORY
DESSERT
CALORIES
350 kcal per serving
TIME
45 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups sponge cake, cut into layers
  • 1/4 cup strawberry syrup

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1.

In a mixing bowl, whip the heavy cream with sugar and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.

Step 2.

Layer the sponge cake slices on a serving dish and drizzle with strawberry syrup.

Step 3.

Spread a layer of whipped cream on top of the cake and add a layer of sliced strawberries.

Step 4.

Repeat the layers, finishing with whipped cream and strawberries on top.

Step 5.

Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to serve. Enjoy!
