Creamy Mushroom Soup

A comforting soup with creamy mushrooms and herbs.

CATEGORY
SOUP
CALORIES
200 kcal per serving
TIME
30 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp parsley, chopped

INSTRUCTIONS

Step 1.

In a large pot, melt butter over medium heat. Add onions and garlic and cook until softened.

Step 2.

Add the sliced mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and become tender.

Step 3.

Add the chicken broth, bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 10 minutes.

Step 4.

Stir in the heavy cream and cook for another 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.

Step 5.

Puree the soup using an immersion blender or regular blender until smooth. Serve with a sprinkle of parsley.
